Biminis

NDT Wakeboard Tower Bimini’s are made from high quality Sunbrella® fabrics and come in many colors. Our biminis are strong, lightweight, and built to withstand extreme conditions. Sunbrella® fabrics resist sun, wind and water exposure and retain their beauty for years. The over the top style bimimi allows for the maximum amout of headroom while retaining great coverage for shade. All NDT biminis are custom made to fit each tower and fold neatly out of the way when not in use.

Highest quality biminis on the market

New Dimension Tower Bimini’s are specifically designed and fabricated to fit your exact make and model of boat tower. Each NDT Bimini is specially designed with easy release brackets which allow the bimini to be folded and secured to the tower for storage. Most aftermarket biminis come with thin aluminum tubing and cheap plastic fittings. NDT uses the highest quality stainless steel fittings and tubing available to ensure our biminis not only look great but function great as well.
